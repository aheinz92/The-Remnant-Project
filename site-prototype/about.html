About The Remnant Project

The Remnant Project is dedicated to preserving, digitizing, and sharing the endangered stories and historical records of Black and Indigenous communities in Washington State at Tacoma Evergreen State College. The remnants of stories, struggles, and triumphs that shaped our cultural, social, and civic landscape. These pieces of our past, often overlooked or devalued, are treasures that deserve to be safeguarded and shared.

Our mission is to preserve these stories in ways that honor the people they come from, using methods that break free from traditional practices that often strip away context and connection, and place them in the hands of the people. We are creating a digital archive, accessible online and in virtual reality, where these stories can be experienced, shared, and celebrated by everyone.
